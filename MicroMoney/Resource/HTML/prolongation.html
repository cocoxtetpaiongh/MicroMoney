Prolongation manual

What should you do if you are unable to pay the money on due date?

Don’t worry. We care our customers and understand their difficulties. you can PROLONGATE your loan and avoid penalties!

  • On how many days you want to prolongate \ to delay your payment?
    Example: you borrow 50 on 10 days. And you want to prolongate on 7 days more.
    Pay now service fees 5000 for time you already use money PLUS service fee 3500 for 7 days that you want to continue using your loan.
    After 7 days you can extend your loan one more time, or fully close your loan and pay 50 only.
    For example you borrow 100 on 14 days. And you want to prolongate on 10 days more.
    Pay now service fees 14000 for time you already use money PLUS service fee 10000 for 10 days that you want to continue using your loan.
    After 10 days you can extend your loan one more time, or fully close your loan and pay 100 only.
  • If you fail to repay full amount or buy prolongation – we will charge you 5,000 prolongation fee. You should pay full amount of your loan + service fees as soon as possible!
  • Remember always try to repay the money with one of the two options mentioned above. If you don’t, we will have to take appropriate legal actions.
